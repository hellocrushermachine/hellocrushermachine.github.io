Small Aggregates Crushing Machine

Small aggregates crushing machine for sale includes vibrating feeder , jaw crusher , impact crusher, vibrating screen, belt conveyor and central controlling system, the designed capacity of aggregate crushing line for sale is from 0-800 tons per hour.to meet customers’ specially requirement, we can also add other equipment such as cone crusher , dust catcher on the production line .

Big materials are fed to the jaw crusher of aggregate crushing line for sale evenly and gradually by vibrating feeder through a hopper for primary crushing. After first crush, the material will transferred to impact crusher by belt conveyor secondary crush, the crushed materials will then transferred to vibrating screen for separating. The parts that can meet standard will be transferred away as final products. while the while parts will be returned to impact crusher, thus forming a closed circuit size of final products can be combined and graded according to customers; specific requirement . We can also equip dust catcher system to protect environment.
