River Sand Making Crusher

VSI sand making machine

River stone is a very common material on the earth, according to its physical characters; it is widely used for construction field as sand. Our company is professional manufacturer of river sand making crusher; we can supply the equipment to process river stone into sand. This machine is called sand making machine.

Our river sand making crusher-sand making machines are divided into VSI sand making machine and B series deep rotor vertical shaft impact crusher. All of them features high crushing ratio, low energy consumption, large capacity.

VSI sand making machine is one of the most advanced impact crushers. Nowadays it introduces high quality roller bearing , which ensure the smooth running of main unit. Personalized design it used in upper cap, this river sand making crusher is easy and convenient to maintain and replace spare parts. Sand making machine is our sole patent using central feeding and central cascade feeding, which can be changed based on customers’ needs. the capacity of it can reach to more than 500 tons per hour.

Note: the capacity is according to the medium hard limestone. The size, humidity level and characters of materials will influence the capacity.

River Sand Making Crusher For Sand Making

The complete crushing line for river stone from our company mainly consists of hopper, vibrating feeder, jaw crusher, vsi sand making machine, vibrating screen, belt conveyor, and central controlling system and so on. The largest capacity of it can reach to more than 1000 tons per hour.
