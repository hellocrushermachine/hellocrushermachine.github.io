Crusher Machine For Feldspar

K series portable crusher plant is one of the crusher machine for feldspar, which is designed based on the conception of fully adapting various crusher condition, eliminating obstacles caused by location, environment, foundation, configuration, consequently providing simple , efficient, low-cost crusher equipment.

K series portable crusher plant is not only a crusher machine for feldspar,but also widely used in mining , metallurgy, construction, chemical industry and so on. And it is very suitable for processing non-explosive, non-flammable, hard and brittle mineral ores, such as granite, iron ore, gold ore, limestone, quartz, feldspar, basalt and so on.

The Features Of Feldspar Crusher Machine

  1. Built with high quality jaw crusher, cone crusher and impact crusher
  2. Feeder, screen, and belt conveyor on board
  3. Tractive steering shaft, convenient for road transportation
  4. Integrated with landing gears, quick and convenient installation on site
  5. Integrated motor and control panel
  6. Reliable performance and convenient maintenance
  7. Customized production and flexible configuration

Belt Conveyor For potash feldspar processing plant

The belt conveyor consists of standard parts and they have the advantages of large transfer quantity, simple structure, easy maintenance and standard parts. they are widely used in mining, metallurgy and coal industry to transfer sandy or lump materials, or packaged materials. According to different potash feldspar processing plant requirement, the belt conveyor can be used singly or with other transferring equipment. the belt conveyors are usually used in temperature between -20℃ and 40℃and the temperature of the materials should be below 50 ℃.

Vibrating Screen For potash feldspar processing plant

Vibrating screen is widely used in the potash feldspar processing plant ,which is used to separate materials into various size for further processing, or for end use depending on your need. the material is separated by passing it through a vibrating “screen box” in which has a number of different sized screens , or meshes, which the materials falls through like a sieve. the material falls onto attached conveyors and finally are transported to different stockpiles. the end products can then ne used in the building and construction industries.
