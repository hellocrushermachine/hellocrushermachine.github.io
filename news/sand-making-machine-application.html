Sand Making Machine Application

Sand making machine is also called crusher. This machine is widely used. It can be used not only in medium hard and extremely hard materials, but also in artificial sand, pre-powdered cement, mine, refractories, etc. Finely broken and superfine work. With the continuous improvement of sand making technology, we are moving toward automation and intelligent development, and open up new roads for the production of aggregates for sand and gravel to meet the broad market demand.

Sand Making Machine Has A Wide Range Of Application

VSI Sand Making Machine is used in various industries such as various ores, cement, refractories, aluminum all-terrain clinker, emery, glass raw materials, machine building sand, stone and all kinds of metallurgical slag, etc. The impact type sand making machine is a kind of With the international advanced level of high energy and low consumption sand making machine, its performance plays an irreplaceable role in various ore fine-breaking equipments. Impact sand making machine design principle and characteristics

The VSI sand making machine is a versatile new-type sand making machine that combines the experience of producing crushing and sand making for many years and combines the advantages of domestic and foreign equipment. The structural design principle of this equipment is the principle of "stone beat stone", which consists of a coarse cavity, a finely divided cavity, an impact-shaped cavity composed of a strikeable adjustable counterattack plate and an arc-shaped plate, and three discharge passages. Hammer head can be used on all sides, with high utilization rate and small waste. The unique adjustment function makes the counterattack board and the screen plate more effective and convenient to adjust, so as to control the material's particle size, so that the required material particle size can reach more than 95%, and an ideal material particle size control and classification can be realized.

The successful development of the multi-function adjustable impact type fine crusher has completely changed the problems of poor grain size and non-standard grain shape in the crushing and sand making industries that have been trapped for a long time, and solved the problem of crushing and VSI sand making equipment with large hammer wear and adjustment. , Difficult to maintain, resulting in high production costs of the defects.
