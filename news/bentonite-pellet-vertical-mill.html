Bentonite Pellet Vertical Mill

Bentonite pellet is widely used in oil exploration, directional crossing, steel casting, metallurgical pellet, chemical coating, compound fertilizer, sizing, rubber, plastic, paper, water purification, moisture absorption agent, pesticide and other fields. When bentonite pellet applied in various fields, it is needed to make the powder into powder. For a long time, bentonite enterprises in the processing of most of the conventional Raymond mill, ball mill, and other equipment, these machines with high energy consumption, low efficiency, poor stability, product size is not controllable, serious pollution, slag discharge difficult, machine bonding and easy blockage problems facing. So more and more enterprises will look to invest in bentonite pellet vertical mill - Emerging annual processing of 20 thousand tons of bentonite milling equipment.

Bentonite in all applications must be after flour milling, the bentonite pellet processing mill which requires the strong support of the mill equipment. With many areas of the different requirements of bentonite, mining equipment industry also continuous innovation improvement of mill, the better effect on bentonite powder processing. Good bentonite pellet milling machine processing of bentonite powder is of high quality, fine powder, into a high degree, is more widely applied. Our bentonite pellet vertical mill is professional bentonite pellet processing equipment, the whole three-dimensional structure, covers an area of small, system, from raw materials roughing conveyors to the milling and final packaging, can become an independent production system, finished powder fineness, sieve rate of 99%, machine transmission device adopts a closed gearbox and belt wheel, transmission smooth and reliable operation.

Uniform the bentonite silo by vibrating feeder transported to the initial breaking machine jaw crusher preliminary broken, crushing the swelling soil by belt conveyor sent to fine immediately counterattack type crusher secondary crushing, fine crushing swelling soil by belt conveyor is sent into the vibration sieve sieve, sieve ceding several different size, different specifications of the swelling soil, to meet the mill grinding particle size requirements of swelling soil by lifting machine will be sent to the storage hopper, the vibrating feeder will be the uniform quantitative continuously fed into the bentonite pellet vertical mill flour machine grinding chamber of grinding, the grinding of bentonite clay powder by the wind blows, the powder selecting machine grading. With bentonite powder fineness with the gas flowing through the pipeline into the new type cyclone powder isolation device, were isolated and collected. Bentonite powder is collected by the discharge port and the conveying device into the powder bin, ready for use. Not qualified by the particle separator after the separation was thrown to the wall, along the tube wall to fall back to the mill. The whole system is operated under the negative pressure, the system dust does not spill over to ensure the spot cleaning.

Bentonite pellet vertical mill is my company after years of painstaking research and development with international advanced level, with a number of independent intellectual property patents of equipment, low energy consumption, high efficiency, the process were also upgraded. Bentonite ore after crushing sodium, by vibration feeder uniform quantitative continuous into bentonite pellet vertical mill host grinding chamber of the grinding. Slag grinding after being blown up, the powder selecting machine grading. According to the fineness of the powder with gas flow through the pipe into the cyclone dust collector, separation and collection. Collect the product through the discharge port and the conveying equipment into the bin. Not qualified by the particle separator after the separation was thrown to the wall, along the tube wall to fall back to the mill. Bentonite pellet vertical mill beneath the base is provided with a slag discharging port, produced in the process of grinding slag and a small part of the raw material is discharged along the discharging port, after removal of iron slag, by conveying equipment return hopper, once again into the mill for grinding. The system runs in the state of negative pressure, stable and reliable system operation, the dust can not overflow, on-site clean and finished powder quality is guaranteed.
