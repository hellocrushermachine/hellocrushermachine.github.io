Mobile Sand Washing Machine

The mobile sand washing machine, also called sand washer, refers to the machine used to remove dust in sand, and improve the quality of sand in the process of silica sand washing plant. In the construction site, sand stone factory, hydropower station and dam site, the sand washing plant is applied to wash paving sand. With the boom of realty industry, the market prospect of sand making industry is so bright that many businessmen have advanced to sand making, and that is why the demand for sand washing machines grows fast. Nowadays, mobile sand washing machine for rent and sale has two types, including the LSX sand washing machine and XSD sand washer.

Typically, in the silica sand washing plant, it has two kinds of silica sand washing machine for sale and rent, that is, the wheel-type and screw-type. When working, the sand washing plant drives the impeller in the water groove to move like a circle transmitted by electric motor and decelerator. Trough the motion of water groove, slags are discharged. As a professional manufacturer of mining equipment, we offers two types of sand washing machine, including the XSD sand washer and LSX sand washing machine. In addition, two types sand washing machines have achieved to the international advanced level for sand and slag pellets.

mobile sand washing machine Application in Silica Sand Washing Plant

As we all know, the mobile sand washing machine is used to remove the dust in silica sand washing plant, and it aims at improving the quality of sand. Nowadays, two types of sand washing machines have been widely used for cleaning materials in the following industries: quarry, minerals, building materials, transportation, chemical industry, water conservancy and hydropower, cement mixture station,silica sand washing plant and so on.

mobile sand washing machine Working Principle

The mobile sand washing machine drives the impeller in the water groove to move like a circle transmitted by electric motor and decelerator, and then mixes, overturns, cleans and discharges the silica sand or slag pellets in the water groove. For example, LSX series sand washing machine adopts the inclination of 15°. Under the water tub, three weir plates form the sediment pool, the screw head is immersed in this pool, the screw is driven by electric motor through reducer rotating continuously, the fresh water is feeding from porous plate at bottom of sediment pool, this machine have three functions of washing, dewatering, classifying.

Technology Advantages and Features

  1. Reasonable structure, easy maintenance.
  2. High capacity and cleanliness of sand.
  3. There are no other wearing parts except the screen mesh.
  4. It features long service life and high reliability.
  5. There is little loss for medium-size, fine sand and stone powder. After being washed, the grade and fineness of sand for construction can achieve to the international standards, such as “sand for construction” and “cobble and broken stones for construction”.
